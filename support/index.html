Support

CleanLens support.

Help for the private, review-first iPhone photo cleanup app.

Email support at support@cleanlens.net. Include your device model, iOS version, CleanLens app version, and a short description of the issue.

Before contacting support

If possible, include screenshots or a short screen recording. Please do not send private photos unless support specifically asks for a redacted example.

Common questions

Does CleanLens delete photos automatically?

No. CleanLens is built around review-first cleanup. You review selected items before deletion decisions.

Does CleanLens upload my photos?

No. Photo analysis is designed to stay on your iPhone. CleanLens does not need to upload your photo library to find cleanup opportunities.

Where do deleted photos go?

Deletion is handled through the iOS photo library flow. If you remove photos, check Recently Deleted in Apple Photos if you need to recover something.

How do I manage or cancel CleanLens Pro?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple. Open the App Store subscription settings for your Apple Account to manage, change, or cancel a plan.
