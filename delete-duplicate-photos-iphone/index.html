Duplicate photo cleanup

How to delete duplicate photos on iPhone.

Exact duplicates are the easiest photo clutter to clean. The key is to use a process that still lets you review before final deletion.

Updated June 8, 2026

Use Apple Photos first

If your iPhone shows a Duplicates section in Photos, start there. Apple Photos can merge or remove obvious duplicate items without installing anything else.

Know what counts as a duplicate

A duplicate is usually an exact or near-exact copy of the same media. This is different from a similar photo. Five shots from the same birthday, meal, sunset, or selfie session may be similar, but they are not always true duplicates.

When a dedicated photo cleaner helps

A dedicated cleanup app can help when the problem is broader than exact duplicates. CleanLens is useful when you want to review duplicate groups, similar shots, screenshots, large media, and other cleanup categories in one place.

A safer duplicate cleanup workflow

  1. Review the duplicate group before selecting anything.
  2. Keep the sharper, better-framed, or more meaningful photo.
  3. Move only the clear copies toward deletion.
  4. Check Recently Deleted if you think you made a mistake.
