CleanLens Support

CleanLens helps you free up space by finding duplicates, similar photos, videos, screenshots, and other likely clutter in your photo library.

Everything stays on your device. CleanLens does not auto-delete your photos. You review items first, add them to Bin, and confirm deletion before anything is removed.

Common Questions

How does CleanLens work?
CleanLens analyzes your photo library on-device to find cleanup opportunities such as duplicates, similar photos, videos, screenshots, and other clutter.

Does CleanLens delete photos automatically?
No. Nothing is deleted automatically. You review items first and confirm deletion before anything is removed.

Does CleanLens upload my photos?
No. Photo analysis stays on your device.

Why do some photos go to Bin first?
Bin is a review step so you can double-check what you want removed before final deletion.
