How to compare any iPhone photo cleaner.

If you are testing CleanLens against Clever Cleaner, CleanMyPhone, Apple Photos, or any other cleaner, use the same checklist every time.

Check what it considers clutter

Exact duplicates, similar shots, screenshots, and large videos are different cleanup problems. A good app should make those differences clear.

Inspect similar-photo groups

Similar photos are not disposable just because they look alike. Look for a review experience that helps you compare before deleting.

Read the privacy details

Before granting photo access, check whether analysis stays on device and whether the app explains its data practices in plain language.

Avoid irreversible pressure

Be careful with flows that push large deletes too quickly. A safer cleaner should keep the final decision with you.
