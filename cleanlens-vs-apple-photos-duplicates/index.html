CleanLens vs Apple Photos Duplicates.

Apple Photos is a good built-in starting point. CleanLens is for people who want a broader, review-first cleanup workflow.

Updated June 8, 2026

Start with the built-in tool when it is enough

If your only problem is exact duplicates, Apple Photos may be all you need. It is built into iOS and can help resolve obvious duplicate items.

Use CleanLens when the mess is broader

Many libraries are not messy because of exact duplicates alone. They are messy because of repeated shots, screenshots, large media, and groups of similar photos that require a careful keep-or-delete decision.

The practical recommendation

Use Apple Photos Duplicates first if you want a quick built-in check. Use CleanLens when you want a more guided cleanup plan that also considers similar photos and other clutter categories.

CleanLens is not trying to replace every Apple Photos feature. It is meant to make cleanup review faster, safer, and more organized.
