Privacy Policy for CleanLens.

CleanLens is designed around private, on-device photo cleanup with review before deletion.

Effective date: May 2026

CleanLens helps users clean up their photo library by identifying duplicates, similar photos, videos, screenshots, and other likely clutter.

1. Information We Access

CleanLens requests access to your photo library in order to analyze your media and present cleanup suggestions.

2. How Your Data Is Processed

Photo analysis is performed on-device. CleanLens does not upload your photo library content to our servers for analysis.

3. Information We Collect

CleanLens does not collect or transmit your photos, personal information, app analytics, or diagnostic information to SaifApps in the current release. Some app state, such as settings, review selections, cleanup progress, and diagnostic logs, may be stored locally on your device to support the app experience.

4. Deletion Control

CleanLens does not delete photos automatically. Users review items first and confirm deletion before any media is removed.

5. Third-Party Services

The current release does not use third-party advertising SDKs or third-party analytics services. If subscriptions are offered, purchases are handled by Apple through the App Store.

If advertising or additional third-party services are introduced in a future version, this policy and the app's privacy disclosures will be updated accordingly.

6. Data Retention

Any local app state, such as review selections or cleanup progress, may remain on your device until removed by the user or cleared by deleting the app.

7. Children's Privacy

CleanLens is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted at this URL with a revised effective date.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact support@cleanlens.net.
